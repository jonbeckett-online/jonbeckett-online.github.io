# Flight Sim Labs

**Website:** https://www.flightsimlabs.com/
**Founded:** 2012
**Headquarters:** Greece
**Platforms:** MSFS 2020/2024, Prepar3D (legacy)

---

## Overview

Flight Sim Labs (FSLabs) is a specialized developer focused on creating highly realistic aircraft add-ons and specialized tools for flight simulation. The company has built its reputation on producing what many consider the most accurate Airbus A320 family simulation available for the Prepar3D platform, and has since expanded to Microsoft Flight Simulator.

FSLabs is known for their uncompromising approach to systems accuracy and their willingness to push technical boundaries. Their products are designed for serious virtual pilots who want the most realistic experience possible, with systems depth that satisfies even real-world Airbus pilots.

The company also develops specialized tools for other add-on producers, leveraging their technical expertise to support the broader flight simulation development community.

## History

Flight Sim Labs was established in 2012 with a mission to create the most realistic aircraft simulations possible. The company initially gained recognition for their Concorde X project for FSX, which showcased their capability for detailed systems programming.

Their flagship product, the A320-X, was released for FSX and later Prepar3D, immediately establishing itself as the benchmark for Airbus simulation. The product featured unprecedented systems depth, including accurate FMGS, realistic fly-by-wire behavior, and comprehensive failures simulation.

In 2018, FSLabs faced controversy over DRM measures included in their installer, which led to industry-wide discussions about software protection practices. The company addressed these concerns and continued development of their product line.

More recently, FSLabs has expanded to MSFS with the A321neo, bringing their expertise to the new platform.

## Community Reputation

Flight Sim Labs occupies a unique position in the community. Their products are widely respected for technical excellence and systems accuracy. The A320-X for P3D remains highly regarded as one of the most realistic desktop airliner simulations ever created.

The 2018 DRM controversy did impact their reputation, though the company has worked to rebuild trust since then. The A321neo for MSFS represents their latest effort and has received positive reviews for maintaining their high standards on the new platform.

Pricing for FSLabs products is at the premium end of the market, reflecting the development investment and target audience of serious simulation enthusiasts. Support is provided through their website and community forums. For users seeking the ultimate in Airbus simulation realism and willing to pay premium prices, FSLabs products remain a top recommendation.
