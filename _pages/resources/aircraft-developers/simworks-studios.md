# SimWorks Studios

**Website:** https://simworksstudios.com
**Founded:** 2019
**Headquarters:** United Kingdom
**Platforms:** MSFS 2020, MSFS 2024

---

## Overview

SimWorks Studios (SWS) is a UK-based flight simulation developer that emerged as one of the early pioneers in creating high-quality aircraft add-ons for Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020. The company has built a strong reputation within the community for delivering well-crafted general aviation and utility aircraft with excellent flight dynamics and attention to detail.

Known for their focus on bush flying and backcountry aircraft, SimWorks Studios has carved out a distinctive niche in the market. Their products typically feature SystemsPulse technology, which provides enhanced failure simulation and realistic aircraft behavior that appeals to simmers seeking deeper immersion.

The studio maintains an active presence in the community through Discord and social media, providing regular updates and responsive customer support. Their aircraft are praised for striking a balance between study-level depth and accessibility for casual pilots.

## History

SimWorks Studios was established in 2019, positioning themselves to capitalize on the launch of Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020. Their breakout success came with the Kodiak 100, which quickly became one of the most popular third-party aircraft for the new simulator.

The company expanded their portfolio with the PC-12 Legacy and the Van's RV series, establishing themselves as a go-to developer for high-quality single-engine turboprops and kit-built aircraft. In 2024, they began supporting MSFS 2024 with native versions of their products.

## Aircraft Portfolio

### Current Products
- **Kodiak 100 Series III** - Their flagship bush plane, available in wheel and amphibian variants with extensive customization options
- **Kodiak 100 Series III Amphibian** - Floatplane version of their popular Kodiak
- **PC-12 Legacy** - Highly-rated Pilatus turboprop with exceptional systems modeling
- **Van's RV-8** - Aerobatic two-seater homebuilt aircraft
- **Van's RV-10** - Four-seat cross-country homebuilt
- **Van's RV-14** - Modern two-seat kit aircraft
- **GA-8 Airvan** - Rugged utility aircraft, available in Basic and SystemsPulse editions

### SystemsPulse Expansion
- Enhanced failure simulation add-on available for compatible aircraft

## Notable Achievements

- First major third-party developer to deliver a high-quality bush plane (Kodiak) for MSFS 2020
- PC-12 Legacy rated 4.76/5 with extensive community praise
- Early adopter of MSFS 2024 native development
- Pioneered the SystemsPulse failure simulation technology for MSFS
