# Supermarine Spitfire Mk.Ia

## The Real Aircraft

The Supermarine Spitfire is the most famous British aircraft ever built and an enduring symbol of the Battle of Britain. Designed by R.J. Mitchell, its distinctive elliptical wings and graceful lines made it an icon of aviation.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Manufacturer:** Supermarine (Vickers)
- **First Flight:** March 5, 1936
- **Introduction:** August 1938
- **Type:** Single-seat fighter
- **Engine:** Rolls-Royce Merlin III (1,030 hp)
- **Max Speed:** 571 km/h (355 mph)
- **Range:** 636 km (395 miles)
- **Armament:** 8Ã— .303 Browning machine guns

The Spitfire remained in production throughout WWII, constantly upgraded to counter new threats. Over 20,000 were built across 24 marks. During the Battle of Britain, Spitfires were primarily tasked with engaging German fighters while Hurricanes tackled the bombers.

## The Simulation

The **Aeroplane Heaven Supermarine Spitfire Mk.Ia** recreates the early-mark Spitfire in Microsoft Flight Simulator.

**Features:**
- Authentic elliptical wing design
- Detailed Merlin engine sounds
- Period-correct RAF cockpit
- Battle of Britain era liveries
- Characteristic handling and agility
- Compatible with MSFS 2020/2024

**Developer:** [Aeroplane Heaven](/aircraft-developers/aeroplane-heaven/)
