# Schempp-Hirth Discus-2c

## The Real Aircraft

The Discus-2c is a Standard Class glider manufactured by Schempp-Hirth of Germany. It represents the pinnacle of unpowered flight performance, with excellent handling and competition-winning performance.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Manufacturer:** Schempp-Hirth
- **First Flight:** 1998
- **Type:** Standard Class sailplane
- **Wingspan:** 15 m (49.2 ft)
- **Best Glide Ratio:** 47:1
- **Minimum Sink Rate:** 0.52 m/s (102 fpm)
- **Max Speed:** 280 km/h (151 kts)
- **Empty Weight:** 235 kg (518 lb)

The Discus-2c is designed for cross-country soaring and competition. Its excellent glide ratio allows pilots to cover hundreds of kilometers without an engine, riding thermals and ridge lift. It's been highly successful in gliding competitions worldwide.

## The Simulation

The **Got Friends Discus-2c** brings authentic soaring to Microsoft Flight Simulator.

**Features:**
- **Accurate Aerodynamics:** Authentic glide performance and handling
- **Thermal Integration:** Works with MSFS thermal simulation
- **Detailed Cockpit:** Complete sailplane instrumentation
- **Water Ballast System:** Functional ballast management
- **Retractable Landing Gear:** Authentic undercarriage
- **Winch and Aerotow:** Multiple launch options
- **Compatible with MSFS 2020/2024**
