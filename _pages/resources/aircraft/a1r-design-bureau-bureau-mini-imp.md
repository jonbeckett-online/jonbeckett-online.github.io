# Mini Imp

## The Real Aircraft

The Mini Imp is a compact single-seat homebuilt aircraft designed for recreational flying. Part of the experimental/homebuilt aircraft movement, the Mini Imp represents the spirit of personal aviation—affordable, simple to build, and pure fun to fly.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Type:** Single-seat sport aircraft
- **Category:** Homebuilt/Experimental
- **Engine:** Small displacement aircraft engine (typically 50-65 hp)
- **Cruise Speed:** 145-175 km/h (90-110 mph)
- **Construction:** Mixed wood and fabric
- **Empty Weight:** Approximately 180-220 kg

Aircraft like the Mini Imp embody the grassroots aviation philosophy—giving pilots the freedom to build and fly their own aircraft without the expense and complexity of certified designs. These tiny aircraft offer surprisingly spirited performance and excellent visibility.

## The Simulation

The **A1R Design Bureau Mini Imp** captures the joy of flying a simple, lightweight sport aircraft in Microsoft Flight Simulator.

**Features:**
- Detailed 3D model with authentic proportions
- Light and responsive flight dynamics
- Simple, clear cockpit layout
- Multiple color schemes
- Ideal for VFR bush flying and exploring
- Compatible with MSFS 2020/2024

**Developer:** A1R Design Bureau
