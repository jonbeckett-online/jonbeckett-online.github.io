# Parallel 42

**Website:** https://parallelfortytwo.com/
**Founded:** 2020
**Headquarters:** United States
**Platforms:** MSFS

---

## Overview

Parallel 42 (also styled as Parallel 42 or P42) is a flight simulation developer that emerged with Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020, quickly establishing themselves as specialists in bush flying and backcountry aircraft. The company is best known for their Kitfox series of STOL (Short Takeoff and Landing) aircraft, which have become favorites among the bush flying community.

Beyond aircraft, Parallel 42 has expanded into utility software and tools for MSFS, reflecting a holistic approach to enhancing the flight simulation experience. Their understanding of what bush pilots need—both in terms of aircraft performance and simulation utilities—has earned them a dedicated following.

The company name references the 42nd parallel, but more importantly represents their position as a key developer in the flight simulation ecosystem, working alongside the community to create products that enhance the MSFS experience.

## History

Parallel 42 was founded in 2020, coinciding with the release of MSFS. The founders recognized that the new simulator's detailed world and improved terrain would create unprecedented demand for backcountry and bush flying content.

Their initial Kitfox releases proved highly popular, establishing Parallel 42 as a go-to developer for bush plane enthusiasts. The company has since expanded their offerings while maintaining their core focus on aircraft that thrive off the beaten path.

## Community Reputation

Parallel 42 enjoys a strong reputation among MSFS bush flying enthusiasts. Their Kitfox aircraft are consistently praised for authentic flight characteristics, quality visuals, and excellent performance on rough terrain. The company is known for responsive customer support and regular product updates. Pricing is considered fair for the quality delivered. Users appreciate that Parallel 42 understands the specific needs of the bush flying community and develops products accordingly. Their expansion into utilities demonstrates ongoing commitment to the MSFS ecosystem.
