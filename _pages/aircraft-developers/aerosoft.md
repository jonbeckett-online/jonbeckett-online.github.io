# Aerosoft

**Website:** https://www.aerosoft.com
**Founded:** 1991
**Headquarters:** Paderborn, Germany
**Platforms:** MSFS 2020/2024, X-Plane, P3D, FSX

---

## Overview

Aerosoft GmbH is one of the oldest and most established flight simulation publishers in the world, serving as both a developer and publisher of simulation software. As a strategic partner of Microsoft Flight Simulator and X-Plane, the company has positioned itself as a market leader in the flight simulation industry. Their brand philosophy centers on "independence, adventure and freedom," with the tagline "THE WORLD IS YOURS" representing their flight simulation division.

The company operates under two sub-brands: Aerosoft Flight for professional flight simulation software and hardware, and Aerosoft Move for ground-based simulation products. With over 30 years of experience, Aerosoft has built a reputation for delivering quality aircraft add-ons, scenery packages, and simulation tools to the global flight simulation community.

## History

Aerosoft was founded in 1991 in Paderborn, Germany, making it one of the oldest game companies in the country. The company has grown continuously over three decades, successfully adapting to new flight simulation platforms as they emerged. ## Aircraft Portfolio

### Current Products
- **CRJ 550/700/900/1000** - Highly detailed regional jet series for MSFS, featuring accurate systems modeling and custom avionics
- **DHC-6 Twin Otter** - Popular bush plane with float, wheel, and ski variants
- **Airbus A330** - Wide-body airliner add-on for MSFS
- **ATR 42/72-600** - Regional turboprop partnership with Hans Hartmann/S&H Software

### Legacy Products
- CRJ Series for P3D/FSX
- Airbus A318/A319/A320/A321 for FSX/P3D
- Extensive airport scenery catalog for multiple platforms

## Community Reputation

Aerosoft enjoys a generally positive reputation in the flight simulation community, recognized for their professional approach to product development and customer support. Their CRJ series for MSFS has been particularly well-received for its systems depth and visual quality. As a publisher, they've helped bring numerous quality products from independent developers to market. Some users note that pricing can be on the higher end, but the company's track record for post-release support and updates is generally viewed favorably. Their active forum community and dedicated support team contribute to their standing as a reliable choice for simulation software.
