# Flight Replicas

**Website:** https://flightreplicas.com/
**Founded:** 2007
**Headquarters:** United Kingdom
**Platforms:** FSX, P3D, MSFS (via conversions)

---

## Overview

Flight Replicas is a UK-based flight simulation developer that has been creating detailed aircraft add-ons for over 15 years. The company specializes in British and Commonwealth aircraft, with a particular focus on classic de Havilland designs and World War II-era aircraft. Their products are known for their visual accuracy and attention to period-correct details.

Flight Replicas has built a substantial catalog of vintage British aircraft that are otherwise underrepresented in flight simulation. From elegant 1930s airliners to legendary Spitfire variants, their products celebrate British aviation heritage across multiple decades.

While primarily developed for FSX and P3D, many Flight Replicas products have been converted or adapted for use in newer simulators by community members, attesting to the enduring quality and popularity of their models.

## History

Flight Replicas was established in 2007 and quickly became known for high-quality British aircraft. The company found success with their de Havilland series, including the Dragon Rapide and various other classic British types.

Their Spitfire family of products became particularly popular, offering multiple variants of Britain's most iconic fighter aircraft. The company continued developing new products through the FSX and P3D era, building an extensive catalog of British aviation history.

## Community Reputation

Flight Replicas maintains a solid reputation for producing quality vintage British aircraft. Their de Havilland products are particularly well-regarded, and the Spitfire series remains popular among warbird enthusiasts. While the company has been less active in the MSFS era, their legacy products continue to be used and appreciated, with community conversions bringing some products to newer platforms. Pricing has historically been reasonable for the quality offered.
